Executive summary

{{.ProspectName}} is a live, trading business with a working booking funnel and a service page that communicates its offering. The problem isn't the business, it's everything surrounding it digitally. The site scores {{printf "%.0f" .Score}}/100 against our audit model. On-page technical basics are largely missing, mobile performance is in Google's "poor" bracket, and the site has almost no off-page authority signals. In parallel, the competitive context is unfavourable: every money query we tested shows competitors dominating the local pack, and the business is not ranking in any of them.

The wins available here are large but they compound. Fix the technical foundation in the first 30 days, push local visibility in the next 30, and move on authority in the 60-90 day window. Priority findings and phased delivery are laid out below.
